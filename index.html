About

I’m a Power BI developer focused on turning messy source data into trustworthy, self-service analytics. My work centers on clean dimensional modeling, maintainable DAX, and dashboards that answer a specific business question rather than just display data.

I care about data governance as much as visuals: every public artifact here is built on synthetic sources, with revealing metadata stripped — the same discipline I bring to handling sensitive data in a production environment.
