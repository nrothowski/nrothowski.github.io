About

I’m a Power BI developer who turns messy source data into trustworthy, self-service analytics. My work centers on clean dimensional modeling, maintainable DAX, and dashboards built to answer a specific business question — not just display data.

I build reporting for finance and operations teams, where accuracy and trust matter as much as the visuals.

I care about data governance as much as design: every project shown here runs on synthetic data with revealing metadata stripped — the same discipline I bring to sensitive data in production.
