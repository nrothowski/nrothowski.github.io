About

I’m a Power BI developer who turns raw data into analytics that teams can trust and run on their own. My work is built on disciplined dimensional modeling, maintainable DAX, and dashboards designed around a clear business question, not a wall of metrics.

I build enterprise-level reporting where accuracy and credibility matter as much as design. That means owning the full path from source to dashboard: modeling the data, tuning the queries behind it, and making sure every number holds up to scrutiny.

Governance is central to how I work, not an afterthought. Every project shown here runs on synthetic data with identifying metadata removed, reflecting the same standard of care I bring to sensitive data in production.
