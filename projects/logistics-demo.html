Logistics & Delivery Analytics

An operational view of a grocery-delivery operation: are orders arriving on time, where is route capacity strained, and which exceptions need attention today?

Data & privacy

Originally built on a live operational database; rebuilt for this portfolio on fully synthetic data. Column names are preserved so the visuals and DAX rebind unchanged, but every value is regenerated — no real, nudged, or proprietary figures appear.

What it shows

  • Where delivery demand concentrates across the service area
  • On-time delivery rate against target
  • Route load and where capacity is strained
  • Delivery exceptions that need attention

How it’s built

Data model

Star schema: a deliveries fact table surrounded by date, region/zone, route, and customer-segment dimensions — sized to answer operational questions quickly.

DAX patterns

On-time percentage, rolling 7-day averages, target-vs-actual variance, and exception counts — written as reusable measures rather than per-visual calculations.

Refresh

Re-pointed from the production database to a synthetic Excel source, then a full refresh with confirmation that no original rows remain. Scheduled refresh keeps the demo current.
